Novels

  • The Beginning After the End

    • Genre: Fantasy, Action, Adventure, Reincarnation
    Plot Overview: This story follows King Grey, a powerful king who dies and is reincarnated in a new world. In his new life as a child, he seeks to uncover the mysteries of his past while navigating a world filled with magic, monsters, and politics. The story focuses on themes like personal growth, reincarnation, and the challenges of being a powerful individual in a world where power dynamics are crucial

  • Shadow Slave

    • Genre: Fantasy, Action, Adventure, Dark, Mystery
    Plot Overview: Shadow Slave tells the story of a young man, who, after being enslaved in a brutal and supernatural world, gains the power to summon and control shadows. As the protagonist advances, he seeks revenge on those who wronged him while exploring the hidden mysteries of his world.

  • Lord of Mysteries

    • Genre: Fantasy, Mystery, Steampunk, Supernatural, Psychological
    Plot Overview: Lord of Mysteries is set in a steampunk-inspired world where various factions battle for power. The protagonist, Klein Moretti, is transported to this world and gains access to supernatural abilities. As Klein navigates this strange new world, he begins to uncover mysterious events, cults, and secrets tied to powerful forces.

  • Chronicles of the Heavenly Demon

    • Genre: Fantasy, Action, Adventure, Dark, Mystery, martial art
    The Chronicles of the Heavenly Demon follows the story of a young man named Jin Mu-Won, who is betrayed and killed by his own master in a world governed by martial arts. However, instead of dying, he is reincarnated into a new body as a person with the potential to become a powerful martial artist

Manhwa

  • Solo Leveling

    • Solo Leveling: Follows the journey of a weak hunter who becomes incredibly powerful after an unusual event. It mixes action, adventure, and supernatural elements, with the central theme of progression and character development. The story has great pacing and a compelling lead character, which hooks readers

  • Swordmaster’s Youngest Son

    • Swordmaster's Youngest Son: This title features themes of reincarnation, revenge, and martial arts. The protagonist is reborn into the body of a young noble and uses his knowledge from his past life to become the strongest swordsman.

  • Nano machine

    • Nano Machine: A science fiction-fantasy web novel about a martial artist who receives a "nano machine" that helps him gain supernatural powers and abilities. The blend of technology and martial arts intrigues readers.

  • Damn Reincarnation

    • Damn Reincarnation: Another reincarnation-based story, where the main character gets a second chance in life and aims to undo past wrongs. The themes of revenge and reincarnation resonate with many fans of the genre.

  • Avengers

    • The Avengers is a superhero team from Marvel Comics, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby. The team first appeared in The Avengers #1 in 1963. The team includes some of Marvel’s most well-known superheroes, such as Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, the Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye. Over time, the team has expanded to include a variety of other characters, both superheroes and antiheroes, from across the Marvel Universe.

  • One Piece

    • The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a "Devil Fruit" that grants special powers. Luffy's goal is to find the legendary treasure known as One Piece, and in doing so, he aims to become the King of the Pirates. Along the way, he forms a diverse crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, and they travel through dangerous seas, fighting powerful enemies and discovering new lands.

  • Star Wars

    • The Star Wars saga is set in a galaxy far, far away, where various groups battle for control and influence. The central conflict revolves around the forces of good (the Jedi, the Rebellion, the Resistance) against the forces of evil (the Sith, the Empire, the First Order). Key characters include Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo, Darth Vader, Yoda, Rey, and others. The franchise explores themes of destiny, heroism, the balance of good and evil, and the fight for freedom
